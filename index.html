Hi! My name is Elisa van Klink

I am a 24-year-old Master's student specializing in Building Technology at the Technical University of Delft, with a background in Architecture. This portfolio serves to showcase my expertise in computational design and digital fabrication in various projects I did over the years during my study.

Beyond the confines of academia and design labs, I find balance and inspiration in the great outdoors. Nature not only recharges me but also influences my design philosophy, encouraging me to create structures that harmonize with their environments.

As an avid korfball player, I bring the same team spirit, strategic planning, and dynamic energy from the field into every project I undertake.

Explore my work and discover how I integrate technical prowess with creative vision to forge smarter, sustainable environments or contact me to get to know me even better!
